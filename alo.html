Project Alo

A child should get a choice.
Why we do it?

As children, we are all exposed only to conventional forms of highly theoretical teaching and subjects like Science & Liberal Arts. While this limits overall development in context of the modern competitive world,when we grow up, we also end up choosing our career with very little information and only a vauge idea of interest about the profession we are commiting ourselves to. As a result, we are frustrated with our work leading to high levels of mental fatigue, mismatch of personal interests and loss of productivity. At alo, we want to solve this problem of a biased exposure a school child gets in our country.

What we do?

We create solutions for school students which gives them a flavour of how modern day professions feel like via hands on engagement. At the same time, the exposure enables overall development of the child in skills essential to rapid progress in the highly competitive society of today.

How we do it?

We create immersive experiences, tailored to a child's psychology, in professions like Engineering, Fashion Designing, Law etc. Our trained experts provide a highly interactive hands on exposure to school kids via use of hardware and software products, and also keep track of a child's development using advanced data analytics. The child is nurtured in skills like active collaboration, design thinking, brainstorming etc. at a very early age, while at the same time having fun experimenting with professions of his or her future.

Why are we different?

1. Innovative product designs and use of advanced data analytics to track intellectual growth of a child. 2. A team comprised of domain experts in engineering, designing, fashion development, law etc. 3. The only team in the country, assisting the existing curriculum via a project based learning approach

Social Impact

Our attempt to provide a child wide exposure to professions and henceforth make a more well informed choice about career, will reduce work stress and anxiety amoung individuals. It will create a more fulfilled society where interests are being pursued, resulting in overall rise in productivity, both qualitatively and quantiatively.

Economical Impact

We employ underpreviliged factory workers in manufacturing of our products, thus providing livelihood to them. Our products also generate revenue which is reinvested in building experiences in other professions and reaching out to more kids via schools. No environmental impact so arrange accordingly.
